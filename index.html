Organizer is a clean, modern, and local-first document scanner for iPhone. Instantly capture files, extract metadata using secure on-device OCR, and structure your records—without ever sending your private documents to the cloud.

Zero trackers or analytics

Designed for Ultimate Utility

No accounts. No server synchronization. All intelligent operations happen entirely on your phone.

On-Device Camera Scanner

Leverage high-precision camera capture to scan receipts, invoices, contracts, or handwritten notes. Features automatic document boundary detection and real-time perspective correction.

Local OCR & Metadata Extraction

Extract text and content structure natively on your device. Organizer local text models auto-fill invoice dates, tags, values, and vendor names without sending a single byte online.

100% Privacy by Design

Organizer does not compile telemetry, track features, or run analytic libraries. The app operates completely isolated, keeping your documents and metadata entirely your own.

Biometric App Locking

Secure access to your records with Apple Face ID or Touch ID lock support. Ensure sensitive files remain unreadable even if your phone is unlocked.

Secure iCloud Synchronization

Sync documents across your iPhone, iPad, and Apple ecosystem securely using your personal iCloud Drive account. Backup and syncing are fully controlled by you.

Instant Offline Search

Search the complete text content of every document you scan. Thanks to on-device OCR indexing, you can query terms inside any receipt or PDF within milliseconds.

Deep Extraction

Structured Data, Instantly.

Tap on any scan to inspect OCR-extracted text, automatic tax breakdown, and smart totals. Organizer tags information intelligently to keep search fast and accurate.

Absolute Control

Personal Sync & Security

Toggle biometric App Lock, configure iCloud Sync to keep your data backed up across devices, and manage on-device text recognition packages directly from settings.

Our Promise:
Your data is yours. Period.

In an era of mandatory cloud accounts and aggressive web trackers, Organizer stays offline. We do not collect passwords, tracking codes, scanning behaviors, or document content.

Everything is compiled, analyzed, and stored locally on your device's filesystem.
