Organizer Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Organizer does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. The app has no accounts, analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking.

Documents and Metadata

Documents you scan, extracted text, summaries, tags, and related metadata are stored only on your device. If you choose to use iCloud Drive or the Files app to sync or back up your documents, that storage is controlled by your Apple account and device settings.

Camera and Face ID

Camera access is used solely to scan documents. Face ID is used solely to unlock Organizer when App Lock is enabled. Organizer does not receive or store biometric data.

On-Device Processing

OCR, document naming, classification, and field extraction run on device. Organizer does not upload your documents or extracted text to a server.
