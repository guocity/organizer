Organizer Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Organizer is built from the ground up to protect your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our iOS application processes data. We believe that your private files and scanning habits are none of our business.

1. Core Privacy Philosophy

Organizer does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. The app has no user account systems, registration requirements, analytics tracking, advertising networks, or third-party monitoring SDKs.

2. On-Device Processing (OCR & Metadata)

All intelligent features inside Organizer run locally on your iPhone using Apple's native CoreML and Vision frameworks:

  • OCR Text Extraction: Text recognition runs entirely on your device's hardware.
  • Metadata Auto-filling: Parsing details (like merchant names, total invoice amounts, and document categories) occurs completely locally.
  • No Cloud Engines: We do not upload your documents or recognized text to any server for processing.

3. Device Permissions

To perform its core utility, Organizer requests access to the following hardware features:

  • Camera Access: Used solely to capture physical documents. Images taken are processed instantly on-device and stored inside the app sandbox.
  • Face ID / Touch ID: Used solely to authenticate you when App Lock is enabled. The biometric matching is handled directly by iOS's Secure Enclave. The app never receives, stores, or transmits your biometric data.

4. Storage & Backups (iCloud)

Your scanned files and metadata databases are stored strictly inside the app's local sandbox container on your iPhone:

  • Local Storage: Files are saved locally. Deleting the application from your device will delete all scanned documents and associated index records.
  • Optional iCloud Sync: If you choose to enable iCloud Sync in settings, your encrypted data is synchronized across your personal Apple devices. This sync is managed directly by Apple using your personal iCloud container; the developers of Organizer have no access to your iCloud sync folder.

5. Third-Party Libraries & Telemetry

We do not integrate crash reporting or analytic libraries (such as Firebase, Mixpanel, or Sentry) that transmit unique device identifiers or device logs. The app remains sandboxed and silent, respecting your internet bandwidth and absolute privacy.

6. Children's Privacy

Since Organizer does not collect, capture, or transmit any data of any kind, the app is fully compliant with children's privacy regulations, including the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

7. Questions

If you have questions regarding this privacy policy, please visit our Support Page to get in touch.
