Frequently Asked Questions

How does on-device OCR work?

Organizer runs text recognition models directly on your iPhone. Text extraction and classification are calculated locally and never uploaded to servers.

Can I sync scans using iCloud?

Yes. You can enable iCloud sync inside settings to back up and update files across your personal Apple devices.

Does the app collect telemetry?

No. The app runs completely offline without tracking libraries, analytics accounts, or reporting mechanisms.
